Step 1: Add current and previous invoice .csv data
- To download invoice data:
	1) Go to Ingram -> My business -> Invoices page
	2) Look for files with names like "2026SINZ0003211" (Note the INZ)
	3) Make sure you have the correct files. This app will highlight the differences assuming "previous" is first chronologically.
	4) Click download next to invoice number
	5) Select template "Invoice_Compare_Data" and click "Customize Report"
	6) Make sure all boxes under Available Data is selected and click "Save template and execute"
	7) Select "CSV (.csv)" and click "Save and Execute"
	8) On Reports page wait for report to be prepared (reload page as necessary) and click "Download"
		- Note that the downloaded file is compressed - please only upload the uncompressed .csv file

Step 2: Add subscription .csv data
- To download subscription data:
	1) Go to Ingram -> My business -> Subscriptions page
	2) Click export near the top right of the page
		

Step 3: Click "Generate" and "Download" after it is done
